גֵּרְשַׁיִם

Without Nikkud: גרשיים
At times called Grashayim (גְּרָשַׁיִם)

Gershayim means: double Geresh.

Used for multi digit hebrew numerals (ח״י = 18, מתש"ח = 1948)

הוּא שִׂרְטֵט עַיִן vs הוּא שִׂרְטֵט עַיִ״: He sketched an eye / an ayin.

In the Tanakh, the Gershayim is placed above the stressed sylable (וַיִּקַּ֞ח, 1. Mo 22:3)
